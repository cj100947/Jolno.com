Jolno Dance

Jolno action

Welcome to the step by step guide of the Jolno dance and action!

1. Position your hands on the back of your head holding each other.

2. Make sure your arms are bent with you elbows facing in front of you.

3. Start flapping your arms make sure while you flap both elbows make contact.

4. Well done!! you have learned the Jolno action!!

Jolno Dance

Please make sure you have learned the action before advancing to the dance.

1. Start doing the Macarena (Macarena video listed below)

2. When the part of the song where you have to combind your hands behind your head start doing the Jolno action
